MedCare — Questionnaire

Student Daily Check — Quick Questions

Please answer honestly. 1 = Never, 5 = Always.

Stress

1. I feel overloaded with schoolwork or assignments.
2. I find it hard to balance studies and personal life.
3. I feel pressured by teachers/parents to perform well.
4. I often sacrifice sleep to complete academic work.

Anxiety

1. I feel nervous before exams or presentations.
2. I worry about what others think of me at school/college.
3. I feel restless when I have to meet deadlines.
4. I avoid certain activities because I fear being judged.

Depression

1. I feel less motivated to attend school/college or classes.
2. I have little interest in hanging out with friends.
3. I feel sad or down most of the time.
4. I feel hopeless about my academic future.

Social & Family

1. I have a good relationship with my parents/guardians.
2. I can openly share my problems with my close friends.
3. I feel supported by my teachers and classmates.
4. I regularly participate in group activities or events.
